Site Plan

Site Name

Victorville Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

Victorville is a city in the high desert of southern California. It boasts a population of over 150,000 individuals.

Scenarios

  • About the industry in the city
  • Who is the director of the Chamber of Commerce?
  • Where are the best tourist sites in the city?

Color Schema

The following colors will be integrated into the website:

  • Dark brown for the header
  • Cream for the header text
  • Light tan for the body background
  • Dark green for the footer
  • Cream for the footer text

Typography

The typography will be simple. All headers will be serif fonts, while paragraph text will be a sans-serif font, such as Arial or Helvetica
