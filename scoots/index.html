Welcome to Scoots, your premier motor scooter rental company on the sun-drenched island of Cozumel! Nestled in the western Caribbean Sea off Mexico’s coast, we’re here to fuel your island adventure with our fleet of motor scooters, jeeps, and side-by-side ATVs. Whether you’re a local, a spontaneous walk-in tourist, a scooter enthusiast, or part of a cruise group, we’ve got you covered with two convenient locations: one near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in el Centro de Cozumel, and another by the Terminal Puerta Maya, perfect for cruise patrons.

At Scoots, we make renting easy, friendly, and fun—leaving you free to soak in the carefree island vibe. Explore beyond the bustling western tourist spots and discover the hidden gems of Cozumel’s southern and eastern shores, where vast coasts and serene inlets await. Ready to ride?
